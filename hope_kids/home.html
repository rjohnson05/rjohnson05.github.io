Welcome to Hope Kids!

We are a ministry that seeks to please God by helping others come to better understand Him. We place emphasis on teaching God’s Word (the Bible) and equipping parents to continue teaching their children throughout the week. If you want a place where your children can learn God’s Word while having a blast, we are the place for you!

Our Location

Hope Kids is a part of Living Hope Fellowship. Located in the Northeast Helena valley, our facililties allow plenty of room to let the youngsters explore the outdoors and be themselves.
